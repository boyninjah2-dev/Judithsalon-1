Our Sanctuary

Where tradition meets artistry in the heart of Nairobi

At Judith Saloon in Kenyatta Market, we understand that dreadlocks are more than a hairstyle—they're a journey, a statement, and a living art form. As master architects and caretakers for locs, we approach each client with reverence for the craft and respect for the cultural significance of locs in African heritage.

Our studio is a sanctuary where your locs receive the dedicated attention they deserve. From the initial consultation to ongoing maintenance, we combine traditional techniques with modern artistry to create and maintain healthy, beautiful locs that reflect your unique identity.

We honor the roots of this practice while innovating with styles that suit contemporary life. Each service is performed with precision, patience, and passion, embodying the spirit of Rastafari culture and African pride.

Our Craft

Each service is performed with the utmost care, expertise, and cultural respect

Installation

Starting the Journey

  • Consultation: Discussing desired loc size, method, and hair type
  • Method Selection: Twist and rip, backcombing, interlocking, crochet method, or freeforming
  • Sectioning: Precise mapping and parting for perfect pattern and aesthetic

Maintenance & Retightening

The Ongoing Care

  • Retwisting/Retightening: Palm rolling, interlocking, or crochet hook method
  • Washing & Conditioning: Specialized techniques for clean, healthy locs
  • Repair: Fixing weak spots, thinning locs, or broken locs

Styling & Artistic Work

Creative Expression

  • Intricate Styles: Loc updos, buns, braids, and crowns
  • Accessories: Rings, beads, threads, or wraps
  • Cutting & Shaping: Silhouettes and fades
  • Color Services: Dyes and bleaches with special care
